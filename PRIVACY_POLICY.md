# Privacy Policy for PUMP Workout

**Last Updated: November 15, 2025**

## Introduction

PUMP Workout ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

PUMP Workout stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **User Profile Information**: Name, weight, height, age, and profile photo
- **Workout History**: Records of your completed workouts, including exercises, sets, reps, and weights
- **Custom Workouts**: Workouts you create and save
- **Scheduled Workouts**: Your workout calendar and planned sessions
- **Favorite Exercises**: Exercises you mark as favorites
- **Statistics**: Calculated metrics like BMI and workout streaks

**Important**: This data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage and is NOT transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

### Information We Process

- **Exercise Data Requests**: When you use the AI workout generator or browse the exercise library, your device sends requests to our API at https://ai-api-bz4x-a-production.up.railway.app to fetch exercise information. These requests do not contain any personal information.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information to our servers
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Payment information
- Location data
- Browsing history
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

## How We Use Information

The data stored locally on your device is used solely to:
- Display your profile and statistics
- Track your workout progress
- Provide workout recommendations
- Enable you to create and manage custom workouts
- Show your workout history and calendar

## Photo Library Access

PUMP Workout requests access to your photo library for the following purposes:
- **Setting Profile Picture**: To allow you to select a photo from your library as your profile picture
- **Saving Photos**: To save workout-related images if you choose to do so

We only access photos when you explicitly choose to select or save them. We do not scan, upload, or access your photo library without your permission.

## Data Storage and Security

- All your personal data is stored **locally on your device** using React Native AsyncStorage
- Data is NOT backed up to cloud servers
- Data is NOT shared with third parties
- If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted
- We use HTTPS encryption for all API communications

## Third-Party Services

PUMP Workout uses the following third-party services:

### Exercise Database API
- **Service**: Custom API hosted on Railway (https://ai-api-bz4x-a-production.up.railway.app)
- **Purpose**: Provides exercise data and AI-powered workout generation
- **Data Shared**: Only exercise category preferences (e.g., "chest", "back") - no personal information

### Expo Platform
- **Service**: Expo SDK and services
- **Purpose**: App development framework
- **Privacy Policy**: https://expo.dev/privacy

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

PUMP Workout does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You can delete your profile data within the app's settings
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data
- There is no data to delete from our servers as we do not store personal information

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
